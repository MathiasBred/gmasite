Peyton the Bulldog

$8.00 + $2.00 Shipping = $10.00

Peyton is an English Bulldog. She loves her family and they love her. They take her wherever they go, and they go many places! But Peyton has a knack for having fun, no matter where she is. One day, they all go camping. Including Peyton, of course. When it was time for sleep, however, the family got a surprise. What could it be?

These four children are my grandsons, and Peyton is their beloved dog. She is a wonderful, caring, people pleasing, and fun loving addition to the family. Making good memories is what it is all about for this family and their furry friend!
