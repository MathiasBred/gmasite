Marshall is an adorable, furry black bear. He lives in the forest with his mother and brother. He is a happy, adventurous bear who loves to explore the world in and around the forest.

One warm, sunny afternoon, Marshall decides to go exploring. He finds himself out of the forest and discovers something he had never known about before.

All photos were taken at Oswald's Bear Ranch in Michigan's Upper Peninsula.
