"For the Love of God" is a Bible Devotional written just for the young! There are many devotionals on the market for adults, but this book is just for children and teenagers. Each devotion reflects a subject of interest or a topic that young people might grapple with. This is a book of 40 daily devotions, because, in the Bible, 40 is a number often used by God.

"For the Love of God" is a special book that children and teenagers may read daily to guide them toward a better understanding and knowledge of the Bible and God.

All illustrations have been done by my grandchildren, and all photos are of my grandchildren, or places we have been together.
