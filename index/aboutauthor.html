About the Author

Grandma Mickey, AKA Vicki Marie Bowen, is the author of several children's books. Writing books for children had been her passion for many years.

Vicki was adopted into a loving family at three years of age and had a wonderful, happy, childhood in Midland, Michigan. She earned elemantary and special eduaction teaching degrees from Central Michigan Univesity, along with a Spanish Bilingual teaching degree. She taught elementary and special education for more than 20 years.

Vicki has four grown children, twelve grandchildren, and several step-grandchildren. Because of this, she knows much about how children think and how they act. Her goal in life is to help them become good, responsible adults and contributors to society.

Vicki enjoys writing, photography, arts and crafts, and most importantly, spending time with family and grandchildren.
